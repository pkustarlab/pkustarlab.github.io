Spatial-Temporal AI Research Lab

Welcome to PKU STAR LAB, the research group led by Prof. Wei Gao, affiliated to the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Peking University. We are focusing on multimedia/multimodal information processing, especially 3D visual intelligence, multimodal intelligence, and applications.

Opening News

Postdoctoral Fellows

The PKU STAR LAB seeks top postdoctoral researchers proficient in: (1) multimedia coding and processing (e.g. 3D point cloud/image/video coding, 3D Gaussian Splatting compression and rendering, quality assessment and enhancenment, immersive media systems, etc.), (2) 3D vision and multimodal learning (e.g. generative AI, world models, 3D reconstruction and generation, multimodal large models, vision-language-vision models, autonomous driving and robotics, etc.). The successful candidate will conduct academic research work at PKU. Please send a full CV with publications to Prof. Wei Gao.


Ph.D. & M.Phil. Students

The PKU STAR LAB is looking for self-motivated Ph.D. and M.Phil. students in multimedia computing and AI. Please send CV with a complete list of your publications and projects, as well as academic transcript to Prof. Wei Gao.
